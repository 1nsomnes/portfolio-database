# Memory is Fractal
Recently I opened my journal from two years ago and started paging through my meticulously and regularly written entries. As I reread, it reinvigorated a feeling of malaise towards our relationship with memory. How is it possible that the person I was ten, five, or one year ago is the same person as today? That is, how is this constant stream of memory give way to a much more complicated and constantly evolving being?
